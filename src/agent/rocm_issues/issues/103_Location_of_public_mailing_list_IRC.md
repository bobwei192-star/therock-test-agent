### 评论 #1 — jpsamaroo (2019-02-07T19:30:36Z)

@gstoner is there a reason why this issue was closed immediately after being opened? It's not clear whether or not a means of non-contribution-related communication is available for anyone interested in discussing ROCm.

Having a mailing list and/or IRC channel available for discussion and support pertaining to ROCm usage would help to stop people from opening issues on the various projects' issue trackers just to ask usage questions. Having a public communication channel is also beneficial to helping the project gain traction with regular users who might want to discuss topics or ask questions that aren't necessarily related to a single Github-hosted project. Without one, it just feels to an outsider like no one actually uses ROCm, since there's no visible community other than just developers and contributors.

I therefore request that we reopen this issue until some sort of communication mechanism (other than Github issue trackers) is provided and documented publicly.
