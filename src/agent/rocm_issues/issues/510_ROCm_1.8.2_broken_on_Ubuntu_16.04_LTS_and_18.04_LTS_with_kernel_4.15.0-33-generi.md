### 评论 #13 — jlgreathouse (2018-08-29T22:49:45Z)

Older versions are also likely to fail for 4.15.0-33; the problem is a compilation error for amdgpu that will be sensitized by the -33 release; older ROCk driver releases will also have this.

If you purge (and maybe removeall) rock-dkms, then boot into your e.g. -32 kernel, and install rock-dkms -- it should properly build the DKMS modules. It might not properly build your initramfs, so after you reboot into -32, try `sudo update-initramfs -u -k all` and reboot again.
