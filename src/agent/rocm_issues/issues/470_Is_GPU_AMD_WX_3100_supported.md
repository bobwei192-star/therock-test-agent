It's a bit odd that the kfd did not print out anything about finding your GPU. Could you try including the following line in your kernel parameters on your next boot:

You can do this by catching Grub at a reboot, going pressing 'e' when you've highlighted your Ubuntu installation, and going down to the line that says `linux    /boot/vmlinuz ...` and adding `amdkfd.dyndbg=+plm` at the end of it. Then press F10.

After booting, please run `dmesg | grep kfd`. I'd like to see this output, if possible. Thank you!
