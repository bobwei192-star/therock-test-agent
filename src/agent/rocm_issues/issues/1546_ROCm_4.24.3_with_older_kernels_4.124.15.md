We do not support old OS versions like Ubuntu 16.04.x and old kernels like 4.x officially now.
Officially supported OS versions are [https://github.com/RadeonOpenCompute/ROCm#supported-operating-systems](url)
Anything other than these oses and kernels might work with ROCm 4.3(or 4.2) but not sure. Official support can not be claimed on these.
As per my experience, most of the times, things might work on old kernels/oses also, So recommend to give a try.
Thank you.
