## 描述

Ubuntu, ROCm 1.9.1 OpenCL-only, two Vega64 GPUs.
hsa-ext-rocr-dev/Ubuntu 16.04,now 1.1.9-9-ge4ab040 amd64 [installed,automatic]
hsa-rocr-dev/Ubuntu 16.04,now 1.1.9-9-ge4ab040 amd64 [installed,automatic]
hsakmt-roct/Ubuntu 16.04,now 1.0.9-8-g238782c amd64 [installed,automatic]
hsakmt-roct-dev/Ubuntu 16.04,now 1.0.9-8-g238782c amd64 [installed,automatic]
rocm-opencl/Ubuntu 16.04,now 1.2.0-2018090737 amd64 [installed]

Testing with GpuOwl, everything works fine on kernel 4.18.8,
but wrong results are produced (easily, systematically) on kernels 4.18.10, 4.18.12, 4.19-rc7 (i.e. any kernel above 4.18.8 that I've tried).

What is interesting is that no error is reported, neither by the OpenCL API, nor by amdgpu in dmesg.
