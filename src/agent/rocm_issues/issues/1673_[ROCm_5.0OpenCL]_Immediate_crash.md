### 评论 #1 — langyuxf (2022-02-17T01:04:07Z)

There is a workaround for this issue. Just override the gfx version.

`$ HSA_OVERRIDE_GFX_VERSION=9.0.2 /opt/rocm/opencl/bin/clinfo`
