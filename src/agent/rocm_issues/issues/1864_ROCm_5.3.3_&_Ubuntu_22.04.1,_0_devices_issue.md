### 评论 #6 — mrkapqa (2022-12-22T08:55:27Z)

@newfla that depends on your hardware, and on what you want to achieve, i guess.
you can list the available usecases with

`sudo amdgpu-install --list-usecase
`

according to AMD documentation there are 2 major usecases

"all-open" and "workstation" , but it seems  "workstation" is suited if one wants to install OpenCL for graphic cards older than Vega10, eg. Polaris based.

https://amdgpu-install.readthedocs.io/en/latest/install-overview.html

ROC_ENABLE_PRE_VEGA=1

` PATH="/usr/local/sbin:/usr/local/bin:/usr/sbin:/usr/bin:/sbin:/bin:/usr/games:/usr/local/games:/snap/bin"
