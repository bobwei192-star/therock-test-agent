### 评论 #2 — schung-amd (2026-01-27T16:22:53Z)

Hi @EAFA0, @Denizzje, we don't have WSL + ROCm support for Strix and Strix Halo yet. Have you seen otherwise in any of our compatibility matrices? For reference the WSL compatibility matrix is [here](https://rocm.docs.amd.com/projects/radeon-ryzen/en/latest/docs/compatibility/compatibilityrad/wsl/wsl_compatibility.html#gpu-support-matrix), and there isn't such a matrix for Ryzen.

We are aware that WSL parity with Linux support is highly desired, both in terms of ROCm version and hardware support. However, there aren't any details available to share on that at the moment.

I'll close this as a duplicate of https://github.com/ROCm/ROCm/issues/4952 for now, but feel free to continue discussion here if you prefer.
