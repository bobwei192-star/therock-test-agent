### 评论 #6 — aoolmay (2022-03-25T21:13:17Z)

ROCm 5.0.2 + tensorflow-rocm works well with 6800XTs and lately 6900XT as well if that's your focus. OpenCL works great. I gave up on experimenting with out of spec software setups, so just do everything as per manual.

Hybrid, but off the shelf water+air cooled cards run days on ridiculous overclocks, which nets +25% performance on some workloads. Mind you i have my workstations in separate room that's air conditioned to 16 degrees Celsius 24/7. Might want to pay a little extra and get one with the highest boost ceiling, if time to completion means money to you it'll pay off in spades.

Poor performance on small parallel workloads though. GPU time doesn't get divided equally, so there's some uncertainty there to work around.
